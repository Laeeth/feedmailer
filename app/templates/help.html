Help

Feedserf.com is a free RSS-to-Email service. You can add as many feeds as you want, and new items will be delivered by email either instantly or at custom defined times and days.

The source code for this website is free software under the APGL license, and hosted on github.com. For feedback, ideas and comments please drop me a line at chris@metachris.org.

Improvements and patches are very welcome. The user interface could definitely need some love, too. Let me know if you have any ideas! :)
