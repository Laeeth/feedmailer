Help

Feedserf.com is a free RSS-to-Email service. You can add as many feeds as you want, and new items will be delivered by email either instantly or at custom defined times and days. The feeds are refreshed every hour.

The source code for this website is free software released under the APGL license, hosted on github.com. Feedback, ideas, improvements and patches are very welcome. Drop me a line at chris@metachris.org.
